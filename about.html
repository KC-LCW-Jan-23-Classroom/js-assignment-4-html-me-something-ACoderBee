Hi I'm Andrea

I'm a baby Boomer. Born and raised in Middle America in a typical middle-class family with 2 parents, a sister, and a dog.

I had an idyllic childhood. I lived on a dead-end street. I ran through the woods, built tree houses, swung on ropes like Tarzan, and walked to school and the neighborhood store by myself when I was in kindergarten.

I have a wonderful son whom I raised alone. I have no grandchildren, yet! However, I have a brand new car with a brand new car payment. I don't see retirement in my immediate future.

I write this as I try to decipher the complexity of Medicare during my Initial Enrollment Period.
