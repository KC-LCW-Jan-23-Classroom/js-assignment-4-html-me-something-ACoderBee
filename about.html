Hi, I'm Andrea

I'm a baby Boomer. Born and raised in Middle America in a typical middle-class family with 2 parents, a sister, and a dog. I have a wonderful son whom I raised alone. I have no grandchildren, yet!

I had an idyllic childhood. I lived on a dead-end street. I ran through the woods, built tree houses, swung on ropes like Tarzan, and walked to school and the neighborhood store by myself when I was in kindergarten.

However, I have a brand new car with a brand new car payment. I don't see retirement in my immediate future. I write this as I try to decipher the complexity of Medicare during my Initial Enrollment Period.

There is no such thing as a free ride for the able bodied. Without self-respect and pride, we are consumed by jealousy and sloth. All branches of the government are accountable and so are we all.
